JioMart

JioMart is an Indian e-commerce company, headquartered in Navi Mumbai, Maharashtra, India, that started as a joint venture between Reliance Retail and Jio Platforms. The company initially focused on online groceries sales before expanding into other product categories such as fashion, home essentials, and lifestyle products.

Link- https://www.jiomart.com/

Grofers Mart (Blinkit)

Blinkit (formerly Grofers) is an Indian online grocery delivery service. Customers of the company use a mobile application to order groceries, essentials and more online. Blinkit's employees then secure the items from their warehouse and deliver the items to the consumer within 10 minutes.

Link- https://blinkit.in/

Big Basket

Supermarket Grocery Supplies Pvt. Ltd., trading as BigBasket, is an Indian online grocery delivery service. The company primarily delivers grocery goods found in convenience stores, home essentials and food supplies to its customers.

Link- https://www.bigbasket.com/
